Our Approach

At Movement Corps Physical Therapy & Wellness, we help you move better. We believe in personalized service to achieve powerful results. All clients are seen one-on-one by a physical therapist who tailors treatments specifically for the client.

We provide expertise to educate and empower clients to ensure their future success. We look forward to working with you!

Patients

We treat all kinds of patients. We specialize in neurological conditions, including balance problems, Parkinson’s disease, multiple sclerosis, dementia, peripheral neuropathy, and stroke. We also see patients of many ages with orthopedic conditions, including pain or weakness of the lower back, hip, knee, shoulder, and neck.

  • Date: Last update 2019-03-23

Insurance

We accept Medicare and other insurance providers. We are in-network for Medicare, but out-of-network for all insurance providers other than Medicare. We will submit all paperwork to your insurance on your behalf.
